# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 20, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 20, 2026

## Introduction

Dotloop Reporter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our real estate analytics and reporting platform (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the Service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Register for an account
- Connect your Dotloop account via OAuth
- Upload CSV files containing transaction data
- Configure commission plans and settings
- Contact us for support

Personal information may include:
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, company/brokerage name, role
- **Authentication Data:** OAuth tokens, session identifiers (encrypted and stored securely)
- **Profile Information:** User preferences, privacy settings, notification preferences

### Transaction Data

When you connect your Dotloop account or upload CSV files, we collect and process:
- **Real Estate Transaction Data:** Loop names, statuses, property addresses, sale prices, closing dates
- **Agent Information:** Agent names, team assignments, commission structures
- **Financial Data:** Commission amounts, splits, payment schedules
- **Custom Fields:** Any additional data fields you choose to import

### Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service:
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Performance Data:** Load times, errors, API response times
- **Audit Logs:** User actions, data access events, security events (retained for 90-365 days)

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:
- Maintain your session and keep you logged in
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Analyze usage patterns and improve the Service
- Prevent fraud and enhance security

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may limit functionality.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### Within Your Organization
- **Team Members:** Users within your brokerage may view shared transaction data based on role-based permissions
- **Administrators:** Brokerage admins can view team data, manage users, and access audit logs
- **Privacy Controls:** You can configure commission visibility settings to control what data others can see

### Service Providers
We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:
- **Cloud Hosting:** AWS (for database and file storage)
- **Authentication:** Manus OAuth platform
- **Analytics:** Usage analytics providers (anonymized data only)

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Court orders, subpoenas, or legal processes
- Requests from law enforcement or government agencies
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety
- Prevention of fraud or illegal activity

### Business Transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption:** Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access control (RBAC) with granular permissions
- **Authentication:** Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with session management
- **Rate Limiting:** Protection against brute force and denial-of-service attacks
- **Security Headers:** CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options, and other protective headers

### Organizational Safeguards
- **Audit Logging:** Comprehensive logging of all data access and security events
- **Regular Reviews:** Periodic security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Employee Training:** Staff trained on data protection and privacy practices
- **Incident Response:** Documented procedures for security incidents

### Data Retention
- **Active Data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Audit Logs:** Retained for 90 days (general) to 365 days (critical events)
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account closure

Despite our efforts, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

## Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### Access and Portability
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your personal information
- **Data Export:** Download your transaction data in CSV or JSON format
- **Transparency:** View audit logs of who accessed your data

### Control and Correction
- **Right to Correct:** Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Privacy Settings:** Configure commission visibility and data sharing preferences
- **Preferences:** Manage notification and communication preferences

### Deletion and Restriction
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Restrict:** Limit how we process your information
- **Account Closure:** Delete your account and associated data

### Objection and Withdrawal
- **Right to Object:** Object to certain data processing activities
- **Withdraw Consent:** Revoke consent for optional data processing
- **Opt-Out:** Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@dotloop.com or use the in-app privacy settings.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

- **Right to Know:** What personal information we collect, use, and share
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices

To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@dotloop.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

- **Legal Basis:** We process data based on consent, contract performance, legal obligations, and legitimate interests
- **Data Protection Officer:** Contact dpo@dotloop.com for privacy inquiries
- **Supervisory Authority:** Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
- **International Transfers:** Data may be transferred to the United States with appropriate safeguards
